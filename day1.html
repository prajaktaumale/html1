YOGA

Yoga is an act that unites the body with the soul. It is means through which we can attain inner peace. The great relaxing effect that yoga has on our minds has innumerable health benefits. It originated in ancient India during the Indus Valley civilization and has grown in popularity ever since.Yoga is essentially a spritual discipline based on extremely subtle science, which focuses on bringing harmony between mind and body. It is an art and science of healthy living. The word 'YOGA' is derived from the Sanskrit root 'Yuj', meaning 'to join' or 'to yoke' or 'to unite'.

Yoga is a technique where a combination of breathing, exercising and meditiation is combined in the right proportion. Yoga helps in increasing the flexibility of the body, aids in strengthering of muscles, and helps in relaxation and calming down oneself. Yoga basically works on different'Anannas'or postures, which focuses for certain body parts combined with paranaynama or breathing technique that helps to integrate the body and mind. There are more than a thousand poses in yoga and it mainly focuses on the mind by teaching you to concetrate on certain parts of the body.
